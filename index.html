Explore

Explore the world and discover new experiences near you. Dive in to a experience and find everything the location has to offer. You may find new secrets and hidden gems!

Collect

While exploring an experience, you will encounter challenges that will reward you with trophies for your shelf. How many experiences can you complete?

Learn

Upon completing challenges, not only will you be rewarded with an awesome trophy, but you will discover fun facts about the challenge. Did you know that the janitor form Scrubs attended Bradley University?

Create

Make your own experience! Customize the challenges. Provide helpful hints for your explorers. Make a rewarding experience perfect for your school or tour group or to share with the whole world!

Wait. How does Shelf Work?

An iBeacon, or a tiny low-powered transmitter, is placed near a landmark or point of interest by an experience coordinator.

The iBeacon uses Bluetooth to communicate with your device.

Once your phone is within range of the iBeacon (about 5 feet) you will get a notification informing you that you have encountered a challenge.
