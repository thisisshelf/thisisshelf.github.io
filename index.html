How does Shelf work?

Shelf uses iBeacon technology to detect your proximity to a challenge

Shelf is mobile application that encourages and rewards users for exploring and learning. Think of a scavenger hunt, a walking tour, or a museum exhibition -- Using iBeacons, Shelf can enhance these adventures by providing a platform for location based challenges. Upon completing a challenge a world of information is unlocked and a trophy is added the shelf.
